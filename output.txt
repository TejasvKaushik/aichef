### Spinach and Goat Cheese Bread Pudding

Approximate time to complete: 1 hour and 30 minutes
Serve: 6

Ingredients:
1 loaf of bread
1/2 cup chopped spinach
1/3 cup chopped goat cheese
1 cup milk
1/2 cup butter
1/3 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 cups corn
1/2 cup grated carrots
1 banana, sliced

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine bread, spinach, goat cheese, milk, butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, salt, and cinnamon. Stir until bread is coated and mixture is well combined.
3. Fold in corn, carrots, and banana.
4. Pour into a buttered 9x13-inch baking dish.
5. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until pudding is set and top is golden brown.
6. Let cool for 5-10 minutes before serving.
